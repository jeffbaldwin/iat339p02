Sensitive Pins' Style Guide

At Sensitive Pins, we are dedicated to creating pins for those sensitive individuals wanting to wear their feelings on their sleeves. Our pins are designed by a selection of artists dedicated to supporting our cause of being open with our sensitive feelings and self. Fifty-percent of proceeds go towards our Sensitive Charity to help individuals with their sensitive needs.
